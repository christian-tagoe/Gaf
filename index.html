Who We Are!


Our mission is not only to impart technical skills but also to in still a sense of entrepreneurship and environmental responsibility in our students. We believe that a well-rounded education in Carpentry can empower individuals to contribute meaningfully to their communities and build sustainable futures for themselves.

Our Facilities

This program involves working with wood to construct, install, and repair structures and items such as furniture, and other wooden objects. We will use various tools and techniques to shape, cut, andjoin wood to create functional and aesthetically pleasing products.
